About

Abro-Collective

Abro Collective, a new small shop with a big personality is now open in Union Station. “Abro”, which means "Together" in Amharic is a collective of seven DC-based artists, makers and curators who have come together to offer one-of-a-kind gifts and a unique shopping experience.

This shop is a must-visit for anyone seeking out one-of-a-kind items from artwork, handmade jewelry, vintage clothes, locally handcrafted therapeutic body products and candles to locally designed streetwear, and internationally sourced handmade clothing and accessories from Ghana, Ethiopia, and Uzbekistan and more.
